The Das Conference

March 30th 2018

We went to the DAS conference, this conference is a meeting for everyone working inside an institution that provides education for laboratory studies. Our goal on this event was to provide information about iGEM through the Netherlands and make contacts with specialist. These specialist gave mini presentations in which we learned about sensors, chips and chemical processes. We also present our top 3 ideas by handing out brochures, in which we received feedback and input for improvement our additions. We were very happy to make contacts and carry out the iGEM competition through the Netherlands.

Nationale carière beurs (National Career fair)

April 6th 2018

Today we went to the National Career Event in Amsterdam. This is a big Dutch event where more than 120 employers gather to expose their company to a bigger audience and share contact information with potential recruits and interns. We had hoped to familiarize some relevant companies to iGEM along with trying to attain some sponsors. Sadly the event was primarily focused on IT companies with a very small chemical company presence and nothing biology related. Despite this it was a valuable team bonding experience.

BCF career event

May 24th 2018

After we went to the National Career Event in Amsterdam, we thought that it might be helpful if we'd go to more events. Today we went to the BCF (biology, chemistry and pharmacy) in Utrecht to promote our project. The BCF career event is an event just like the one in Amsterdam but only for biology, chemistry and pharmacy. It was a nice day where we shared a lot of our business cards with the present companies.
